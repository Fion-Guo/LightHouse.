What's the difference between a dog review and dog notes?
A dog review allows you to share feedback with LightHouse Support about a specific pet you cared for during a stay. This allows us to reference a pet's history to ensure safe pet sitting experience on LightHouse for sitters and their client's pets. Pet reviews are not viewable to you, your client, or other sitters. Once you submit a pet review, you will only be able to see the star rating. The written portion will only be visible to the Rover Support team. Dog notes are a great resource for sitters who want to keep a record of information about a dog that they can reference later. Dog notes are only available to you, and you can reference them at any time from the dog's profile.
How do I get paid for bookings?
  • 1. Tap the menu or More icon in the LightHouse app.
  • 2. Tap Payments.
  • 3. Tap Withdraw Money.
  • 4. Choose your payment method: PayPal or check. Payments redeemed via PayPal are released within 1-4 days, with no fees. Checks take up to 5 business days to process, up to 20 days to arrive, and incur a $5 fee.
  • 5. Once you’ve chosen your payment method and filled out the form, tap Submit.
  • 6. Tap Request Payment.
