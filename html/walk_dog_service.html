How to find the perfect dog walker

When nature calls, our dog walkers are here to answer You can’t always predict a crazy day at work, but you can anticipate your dog’s needs. Instead of rushing home at lunch, book a dog walker to give your dog a 30-minute dog walk. Your dog walker can stop by as many times as you need—on whatever days you need them.

Get detailed updates

With the Lighthouse app, get a comprehensive Rover Card from your dog walker that includes: Start and stop times A map of their walk with total distance Pee, poo, food, and water breaks Cute photos and a personalized note!
